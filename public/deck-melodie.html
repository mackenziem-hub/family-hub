Welcome to Family Hub, Melodie

Mac built this for the two of you. It's your family's own private helper — groceries, the calendar, meals, the kids, and an assistant that actually knows your family. Here's the quick tour.

What it is

One calm place for the family

Instead of five apps and sticky notes, it's one spot you can just talk to. Ask it anything, and it can actually do things — add to the list, check the week, plan a meal, suggest something for the kids.

Just talk to it (Home tab)

Type, or tap the mic and speak. It can:

  • Add to the grocery list ("we're out of milk and eggs")
  • Tell you what's coming up this week
  • Log a meal or your activity
  • Suggest an activity for Pepper or Jack
  • Find a time you and Mac are both free
✨

Capture anything

Got a school email, an invite, or a photo of a flyer? Paste it or snap it in the Capture box on Home — it pulls out the events, groceries, and to-dos for you. You just review and tap "Add."

Helpful modes

Ask a specialist

Tap a chip on Home to focus the help — Nutrition, Healthy living, or Coach. It's always gentle and encouraging, never pushy, and it keeps the kids' wellbeing front and centre.

📅

Connect your calendar (once)

Calendar tab → Connect Google Calendar → tap through the "Continue" screen. After that it can see when you and Mac are both free and add events for you. One-time setup.

🧸

It knows Pepper & Jack

Ask for activity or conversation ideas and they come back tuned to each of them — Pepper's reading and crafts, Jack's dinosaurs and drawing — with a warm, strengths-first approach. Never a lecture, always an idea to try.

🎨

Make it yours

  • You tab → tap 🎨 to pick a look you like
  • Fill in a little about yourself, or tap "Interview me" and just chat
  • Snap a photo of the pantry/fridge so meal ideas fit what you actually have

The more it knows you, the better it helps — and it learns as you go.

🔒

It's just yours

Private to you and Mac. Your family's information stays yours — it's not a data broker's, and it's not connected to the business side of anything.

🌿

That's it — have fun with it

Talk to it like a person. There's no wrong way to use it, and it remembers, so it only gets more helpful. Anything confusing, just ask Mac.
